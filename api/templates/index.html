How Match Scores are Calculated Semantically

1. Skill Match: The system matches your selected skills against the skills required by the job vacancy in the ontology. The match percentage is calculated as: Score = (Number of Matched Skills / Number of Required Skills) * 100.

2. Academic Eligibility: The system verifies your education level against the job's minimum required degree level. If the job requires a Bachelor's but you only hold a Diploma, an Education Mismatch is flagged, preventing an Excellent/Good match ranking.
